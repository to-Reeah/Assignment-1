My Biography

My name is Njoku Victoria Amarachi, I hail from Imo state of Nigeria. I am igbo by tribe and i come from a family of four children of which I'm the first. I live in the Nothern part of Nigeria, Kaduna to be prescise where i stay with my parents.

I completed my nursery school in the year 2003 from NAOWA and then Command children School, Dalet barracks where I did my primary school from the year 2003-2008 and then went on to secondary school at Airforce Secondary School Kaduna, from 2008-2014 afterwards I had a little pause and from 2017-2018 I attended ABU College of Agriculture and Animal Science also here in Kaduna and now I'm studying Cloud Engineering at Altschool Africa.

I am a lover of good food and good music, i listen to music most of the time and im also a member of my church choir, I also enjoy cooking good food. I hardly read books, I enjoy podcasts instead. I love meeting new people and making friends, sometimes I enjoy talking most times i don't.
